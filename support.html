Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a property?

Tap the Properties tab at the bottom of the screen, then tap + Add in the top right. Enter the property name and address, then add or customise the rooms for that property.

How do I start a new inspection?

Tap the Inspections tab, then tap + New. Select the property you want to inspect, then work through each room — setting a rating and adding any notes or photos.

How do I add photos to a room?

When editing a room during an inspection, tap the camera / photo button. You can take a new photo with your camera or choose an existing one from your photo library.

How do I email an inspection report?

Open any completed inspection, then tap the envelope icon in the top right corner. The app will generate a professional PDF report and open your mail app ready to send.

How do I edit an existing inspection?

Open the inspection from the Inspections list and tap Edit in the top right. You can update ratings, notes, and photos, then tap Done to save your changes.

Where is my data stored?

All your data — properties, inspections, photos — is stored in your own private iCloud account. It is never shared with or accessible by anyone else, including the developer.

Is there a limit on how many inspections or photos I can store?

There is no limit set by the app. Storage is only limited by your available iCloud space, which you can manage in your iPhone's Settings under your Apple ID.

Will my data sync across my devices?

Yes. Because the app uses iCloud, your inspections and properties will automatically appear on any iPhone or iPad signed in to the same Apple ID.

I deleted an inspection by mistake — can I recover it?

Unfortunately, deletions are permanent and cannot be undone within the app. If you have an iPhone backup, you may be able to restore a previous version through iCloud or iTunes.
