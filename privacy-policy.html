Privacy Policy

Property Inspector Pro

Overview

Property Inspector Pro ("the App") is developed and operated by Ross Milner ("I", "me", or "my"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information. I am committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about my practices.

Short version: All your data stays in your own iCloud account. I never see it, collect it, or have access to it.

Data Storage

All data you create in Property Inspector Pro — including property details, inspection records, room ratings, notes, and photos — is stored exclusively in your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit private database.

This means:

  • Your data is stored in your own iCloud, not on any server I own or control.
  • I have no access to your data at any time.
  • Your data is protected by Apple's security and encryption.
  • Your data is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy in addition to this one.

Data I Do Not Collect

I do not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Location data
  • Photos or inspection content
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data
  • Crash reports or diagnostics (beyond what Apple may collect separately)
  • Any data from third-party advertising or tracking networks

The App does not contain any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising frameworks.

Camera and Photo Library

The App requests access to your device's camera and photo library solely so you can attach photos to room inspections. This access is used only when you actively choose to add a photo. Photos you capture or select are stored only within your own iCloud data (via CloudKit) and are never transmitted anywhere else.

PDF Reports and Email

The App can generate PDF inspection reports and open your device's built-in mail app to send them. All PDF generation happens entirely on your device. I do not receive copies of any reports you send, nor do I have access to your email contacts or messages.

Children's Privacy

Property Inspector Pro is intended for adults (landlords and property managers). The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and I do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If I make material changes to this Privacy Policy, I will update the "Last updated" date below. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
